Hello, I'm Iza

A graphic designer with 5 years of freelance experience and a deep love for visual storytelling. My creative journey started with a passion for art, and it has grown into a continuous practice of design exploration and problem-solving. Over the years, I’ve specialized in logo design and learned the importance of understanding client briefs, adapting to feedback, and turning ideas into visuals that truly speak.

Now, as a computer science graduate, I’m expanding my creative skillset into front-end web development — where design meets function. With a growing understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I’m blending my design eye with responsive, user-focused development. I believe good design is not only aesthetic but also communicates, adapts, and connects.

Curious, adaptable, and committed to growth — I’m always open to new challenges, fueled by research and driven by the desire to make every visual meaningful.

Nutrifuse

Nutrifuse is a cold-pressed juice brand based in Magelang, Indonesia, offering pure fruit extracts without added water or preservatives.

Available in three refreshing variants — Scarlet, Greenish, and Sunset — Nutrifuse is best enjoyed chilled for maximum flavor. The brand features a lettermark logo combining the initials N and F, enhanced with a green color palette that symbolizes freshness and nature.
